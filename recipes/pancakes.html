Whole wheat pancake mix

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe, very easy to make!

Enjoy your delicious breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoom baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Directions

  1. Whisk all ingredients and store in an airtight container
  2. Mix 1 cup of mix, 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, 2 tablespoons of oil in a large bowl. Cook pancakes, serve with your choice of toppings
